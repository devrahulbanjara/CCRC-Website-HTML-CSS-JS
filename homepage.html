Who We Are

Capital College and Research Center, founded in 2002 A.D., stands as an independent institution committed to providing superior education at the higher secondary level. Specializing exclusively in Plus-2 level courses, our institution is affiliated with the Nepal Examination Board (NEB), Government of Nepal. We take pride in our dedicated faculty, student-centric approach, and a curriculum designed to equip students with the skills and knowledge necessary for their academic journey.

The higher education facilities are located within the campus of the main complex of the high school at Balkumari,Kathmandu, which is just 500 meter away from Koteshwor.
